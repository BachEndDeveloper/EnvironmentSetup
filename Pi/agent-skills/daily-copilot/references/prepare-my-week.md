# Prepare my week

Typically run on Fridays, over the weekend, or Monday morning. Call
`list_tasks` across all stream lists before starting. M365 reads go through
`m365-martin`; task writes follow `references/todo-conventions.md`. Produce the
four sections below, in order.

## 1. Open questions blocking prep

List the meetings, messages, decisions, or answers that need something from
Martin before weekly prep can continue. Ask each question directly so he can
answer inline.

## 2. Weekly milestones

Propose three to five milestones for the week, ordered by impact × priority.
For each:

- Milestone — one line
- Why it matters — one line
- What "done" looks like — one line

Once Martin confirms, create them in To Do as `[M] WNN: <outcome>` tasks in
the appropriate stream list. Fill body Context with "why it matters / DoD",
set due_date to end of week (or the real target if harder), importance `high`,
and seed steps with the DoD checklist. For any milestone with a hard external
deadline, also propose a Calendar event. (Milestone shape:
`references/todo-conventions.md`.)

## 3. Weekly calendar scan

- Total meeting hours
- Focus-time blocks available (candidates for `deep` work)
- Conflicts or gaps worth adjusting
- Travel, off-sites, or anything non-standard — including handball
  commitments if they clash with work

## 4. Preparation ahead

Meetings later in the week that need prep — list them and propose when to do
the prep. Offer to add the prep as a To Do task on the relevant day (category
`deep` if it needs focused attention, with a reminder set), and as a Calendar
block if the prep needs a protected window.
